Troubleshooting — Common Issues & Solutions

Issue: Controller won't connect

  1. Turn the controller off and on: press and hold the Xbox button for 6 seconds to turn off, then press again to turn on.
  2. Replace batteries with fresh AA cells or charge the pack.
  3. Attempt wired connection with a USB-C cable. If wired works, re-run wireless pairing.
  4. Remove the device from Bluetooth list and re-pair from scratch.

Issue: Buttons or sticks are unresponsive

  1. Reconnect the controller (turn off/on or unplug and plug if wired).
  2. Check for debris around buttons and clean gently with compressed air.
  3. Update controller firmware via Xbox Accessories app (Windows) or console settings.

Issue: Headset audio not working

  1. Verify headset is seated firmly in the 3.5mm jack (if applicable).
  2. Check console or PC audio output settings and volume.
  3. Try another headset to rule out headset hardware failure.

When to seek additional help

If basic steps fail, visit official support pages or contact support. Record the controller behavior, steps taken, and screenshots if possible before contacting support.
