User Guide — Setup & Basic Use

Tools & Materials Needed

  • 2× AA alkaline batteries or a rechargeable battery pack
  • USB-C cable (if charging or wired connection is needed)
  • Xbox console or Windows 10/11 PC (Bluetooth or wireless adapter for wireless connection)

Setup — Step by step

  1. Insert batteries.

    Open the battery compartment on the back and place two AA batteries. Close the cover.

  2. Turn on the controller.

    Press and hold the Xbox button in the center for 1–2 seconds until it lights up.

  3. Connect to a console or PC (wireless).

    On Xbox: press the controller's Pair button and the console's Pair button. On Windows PC: open Bluetooth settings and pair "Xbox Wireless Controller".

  4. Connect via USB (wired).

    Plug a USB-C cable into the controller and the other end into the console or PC. The controller will work as a wired device and charge if using a rechargeable pack.

  5. Adjust settings.

    Open the console or PC settings to remap buttons, update firmware, or adjust vibration and audio levels.

Everyday Use — Quick actions

  • To mute headset: press the mute button (if available) or use the Xbox accessory menu.
  • To check battery: press the Xbox button once; the controller light may show battery status in the UI.
  • To turn off: press and hold the Xbox button for 6 seconds or turn off from the console menu.

Control Labels

All images in this documentation include clear labels. Use the ABXY buttons for actions, thumbsticks for movement and camera control, and triggers for analog actions like acceleration and aiming.
