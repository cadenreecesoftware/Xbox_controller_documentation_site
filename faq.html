Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How do I know if my controller is connected?

Press the Xbox button. If it lights and the console or PC shows the controller as active, it is connected. For wireless connections, a solid light usually indicates a link.

Can I use the controller on a PC?

Yes. Use Bluetooth (if your PC supports it), the Xbox Wireless Adapter, or a USB-C cable for a wired connection.

Which batteries should I use?

Use two AA alkaline batteries for best single-use convenience, or use an official rechargeable battery pack for repeated charging.

Does the controller need firmware updates?

Occasionally. Connect the controller to your console or the Xbox Accessories app on Windows to check for and install firmware updates.

Where is the Pair button?

The Pair button is on the top of the controller near the USB-C port. Press and hold it to start wireless pairing mode.
